Research

I'm interested in generative AI, large language models, graph neural networks, and deep learning. Most of my research is about developing AI-based models for drug discovery (molecule generation and optimization, chemical reaction yield prediction). Some papers are highlighted.

log-RRIM: Yield Prediction via Local-to-global Reaction Representation Learning and Interaction Modeling
Xiao Hu, Ziqi Chen, Daniel Adu-Ampratwum, Xia Ning
Journal of Chemical Information and Modeling, 2025
arXiv / code

A graph transformer framework for chemical reaction yield prediction with cross-attention between reagents and reaction centers, achieving superior performance especially for medium-to-high yield reactions.

GeLLM³O: Generalizing Large Language Models for Multi-property Molecule Optimization
Vishal Dey*, Xiao Hu*, Xia Ning (*equal contribution)
Association for Computational Linguistics (ACL), 2025
arXiv / code

Introduced MuMOInstruct, the first large-scale instruction-tuning dataset for multi-property molecule optimization with 255K+ pairs across 63 tasks. Developed GeLLMO models with strong zero-shot generalization to unseen optimization tasks.

Large Language Models for Controllable Multi-property Multi-objective Molecule Optimization
Vishal Dey, Xiao Hu, Xia Ning
Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing (EMNLP), 2025
arXiv / code

Introduced C-MuMOInstruct for multi-property optimization with explicit controllable property-specific objectives. GeLLMO-Cs achieve up to 126% higher success rate with impressive zero-shot generalization.

Deep Joint Source-Channel Coding with Iterative Source Error Correction
Changwoo Lee, Xiao Hu, Hun-Seok Kim
International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Statistics (AISTATS), 2023
arXiv / code

Proposed an iterative source error correction (ISEC) decoding scheme for deep joint source-channel coding, improving distortion and perceptual quality metrics over conventional one-shot decoding baselines.
